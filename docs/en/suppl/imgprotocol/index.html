Image acquisition protocol

The way the pictures of antibiograms are acquired can facilitate the automatic reading and minimize the risk of errors. In order to standardize picture acquisition we have developped a simple image acquisition protocol. This protocol has been designed with simplicity in mind: it can be implemented with cardboard and simple material tipically available in every laboratory.
